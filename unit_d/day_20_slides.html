Building a Visual Identity

Brand serves function, not just aesthetics

Visual identity is a system of decisions

  • Typography creates hierarchy and scannability
  • Color creates meaning and draws attention
  • Spacing creates rhythm and breathing room
  • Consistency builds trust — inconsistency feels broken

The question isn't "What looks cool?" — it's "What serves this person?"

Same category.
Completely different visual identities.

What does each visual identity communicate about who the product is for?

A hierarchy is a system of roles

  1. Display — App name, splash screens (largest, most expressive)
  2. Heading — Section headers, screen titles (clear, bold, scannable)
  3. Body — Descriptions, content (readable, comfortable)
  4. Caption/Label — Metadata, timestamps (small, unobtrusive)
  5. Button — Interactive elements (medium weight, high contrast)

Every size needs a purpose. If you can't name its role, you don't need it.

Pairing typefaces

  • Contrast: pair a serif with a sans-serif, or a geometric with a humanist
  • Avoid similarity: fonts that are too close look like a mistake
  • One expressive + one workhorse is usually enough
  • Match your persona: playful vs. professional, warm vs. clinical

Type scale: Start from body text (16–18px), use a ratio (1.25 or 1.333), assign a role to each level.

Open your phone's Settings app. How many distinct type styles? What role does each serve?

AI-Assisted Brand Exploration

Generate options fast, evaluate them carefully

AI generates — you evaluate

What AI can do

Generate color palettes, suggest type pairings, create quick mockups, brainstorm brand names and directions

What AI can't do

Understand your specific users, evaluate cultural context, feel the emotional weight of a color, judge what's distinctive vs. generic

Does this palette have 4.5:1 contrast? Does it match your persona? Would your user trust this?

Write your 3 brand personality adjectives

  1. Write 3 adjectives that describe the personality your app should communicate
  2. Write a prompt asking AI for color palette suggestions using those adjectives + your persona description
  3. Save this — you'll use it in the work session
